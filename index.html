Illuminate the Black Box with Kryptospect

By leveraging state-of-the-art machine learning, we've built a system that learns the fundamental "fingerprints" of cryptographic operations directly from binary code.

Our AI models are trained on a massive, multi-architecture dataset, enabling them to generalize and identify cryptographic patterns even in unfamiliar or obfuscated binaries. Kryptospect automates what was once a manual, painstaking process, empowering analysts to assess firmware security at unprecedented speed and scale.

A Comprehensive Toolkit

Kryptospect is equipped with a suite of powerful features designed to provide a complete and granular view of the cryptographic landscape within any firmware binary.

Multi-Architecture Support

Analyze firmware from a wide range of ISAs including ARM (32/64), MIPS, RISC-V, AVR, and x86 with a single, unified engine.

Primitive Identification

Accurately detect and classify dozens of cryptographic primitives, from ciphers (AES, RSA) to hashes (SHA, MD5) and beyond.

Protocol Pattern Recognition

Our graph-based models don't just find functions; they identify high-level protocol structures like TLS handshakes and key exchanges.

Proprietary vs. Standard

Distinguish between well-known standard algorithms and potentially proprietary implementations that require further manual scrutiny.

Vulnerability Insights

Flag the use of outdated or weak cryptographic primitives (e.g., DES, MD5) and known insecure algorithm parameters.

Interactive Reporting

Generate detailed, interactive reports with data visualizations, code cross-references, and exportable results for deeper analysis.
